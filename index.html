Frequently Asked Questions

Is PlayLax really free?

+

Yes! PlayLax is completely free to download and use. No hidden fees, no subscriptions. We're committed to making lacrosse more accessible for everyone.

How do I check in to a wall or net?

+

Simply go to the wall or net physically, tap the big yellow "+" button on the map, and tap "Check In"! It takes about 30 seconds. You must be at the location to submit it, ensuring accuracy and preventing fake submissions. You can leave a review after checking in.

What if there are no walls or nets near me?

+

Be the first! Add your local walls and nets to help grow the community. Every wall you submit helps dozens of other players find a place to hit the wall.

How do groups work?

+

You can create a group with a password. Share the password with your team, and everyone's check-ins automatically contribute to the group total. Perfect for tracking off-season work.
